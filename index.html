BetterDex Instructions

Blue Circle Button D-Pad
This button will search for the pokemon data after you put a name, or number that is a Pokemon You can toggle through the next or previous Pokemon in the following way: Up-next pokemon Down-previous pokemon Left-back by 10 Right-forward by 10
Blue Square Button (left) Search Field
This button will display card data for the current pokemon You enter a valid number or name to search for pokemon here.
Red Power Button (top left) Yellow Instructions Button (top left)
Will power the BetterDex and open it, or power it off and close the BetterDex This button will allow you to see these instructions again.
