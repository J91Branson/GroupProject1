BetterDex Instructions

Blue Circle Button D-Pad
This button will search for the pokemon data after you put a name, or number that is a Pokemon You can toggle through the next or previous Pokemon in the following way: Up-next pokemon Down-previous pokemon Left-back by 10 Right-forward by 10
TCG Button Poke-Data Button
This button will display card data for the current pokemon This will display pokemon data for current pokemon
Power Button Search Field
Will power the BetterDex and open it, or power it off and close the BetterDex You enter a valid number or name to search for pokemon here.
